# Privacy Policy

**Developer:** One Job Apps Studio  
**App:** Workout Me  
**Effective date:** March 3, 2026

One Job Apps Studio built Workout Me as a simple workout timer/planner app. This Privacy Policy explains what data is processed when you use the app.

## Data we collect
Workout Me does **not** require account sign-in and does not collect personal profile information such as name, email address, or phone number inside the app.

## Data stored on your device
Workout Me stores workout data locally on your device so core features work:
- workout templates you create or edit
- workout progress/session state used for resume functionality
- app preferences (for example, sound and voice settings)

This local data is not shared with One Job Apps Studio by default.

## Analytics, ads, and tracking
Workout Me does not use third-party ads and does not use cross-app tracking.

## Network services
Workout Me is designed to work primarily on-device. If this changes in future versions, this policy will be updated.

## Notifications
If notification features are enabled in a future app version, they are local workout-related notifications only.

## Children’s privacy
Workout Me is not directed to children under 13.

## Changes to this policy
We may update this policy from time to time. The "Effective date" above reflects the latest version.
