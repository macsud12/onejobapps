# Privacy Policy

**Developer:** Maksim Alipov  
**App:** Last Hop  
**Effective date:** March 11, 2026

One Job Apps Studio built Last Hop as a beer exploration app. This Privacy Policy explains what data is processed when you use the app.

## Data we collect
Last Hop does **not** require account sign-in and does not collect personal profile information such as name, email address, or phone number inside the app.

## Data stored on your device
Last Hop stores data locally on your device so core features work

This local data is not shared with One Job Apps Studio by default.

## Analytics, ads, and tracking
Last Hop does not use third-party ads and does not use cross-app tracking.

## Network services
Last Hop is designed to work primarily on-device. If this changes in future versions, this policy will be updated.

## Notifications
Last Hop does not use notifications

## Children’s privacy
Last Hop is not directed to children under 13.

## Changes to this policy
We may update this policy from time to time. The "Effective date" above reflects the latest version.
