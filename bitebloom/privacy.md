# Privacy Policy

**Developer:** Maksim Alipov  
**App:** Bite Bloom  
**Effective date:** March 3, 2026

One Job Apps Studio built Bite Bloom as a simple food discovery app. This Privacy Policy explains what data is processed when you use the app.

## Data we collect
Bite Bloom does **not** require account sign-in and does not collect personal profile information such as name, email address, or phone number inside the app.

## Data stored on your device
Bite Bloom stores data locally on your device so core features work
- progress cards

This local data is not shared with One Job Apps Studio by default.

## Analytics, ads, and tracking
Bite Bloom does not use third-party ads and does not use cross-app tracking.

## Network services
Bite Bloom is designed to work primarily on-device. If this changes in future versions, this policy will be updated.

## Notifications
Bite Bloom use notifications only once a month for new challenges.

## Children’s privacy
Bite Bloom is not directed to children under 13.

## Changes to this policy
We may update this policy from time to time. The "Effective date" above reflects the latest version.
